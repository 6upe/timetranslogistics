Company Overview

About Time Trans Logistics

Company Overview Time-Trans, a Zambian-registered service provider, specialising in delivering bus transportation services committed to providing passengers with secure, dependable, and enjoyable travel experiences to diverse destinations. Renowned in the transportation sector, Time-Trans has built its reputation on punctuality and ensuring customer contentment.

Mission Statement: "To provide safe, efficient, and convenient transportation solutions while prioritising customer satisfaction and fostering community engagement."

Future Initiatives: Time-Trans is dedicated to expanding its routes, enhancing passenger experience through technological advancements, and exploring eco-friendly initiatives to contribute to a sustainable environment.

Services

We Offer the Following Services

Scheduled Routes:

Time-Trans operates an extensive network of routes connecting major cities and towns, catering to short and long-distance travel needs.

Comfort and Convenience:

Passengers enjoy modern amenities onboard, including reclining seats, Wi-Fi connectivity, on-board entertainment, and climate control for a pleasant journey.

Charter Services:

As well as scheduled routes, Time-Trans provides charter services for groups, events, corporate outings, and private functions.

Safety Measures:

Time-Trans prioritises passenger safety, employing skilled drivers, maintaining well-equipped buses, and adhering to strict safety protocols.

  • Reliability:
